On the Subject of Ceasar

Communication was dangerous back in the days. Can you figure out what the original message was?

Decipher the characters on the display with the help of ceasars tactics. Each battery offsets the characters by one negative. There's no delete button so press those buttons carefully!

For example: if the offset is -2, D becomes B and L becomes J. The tabels below can be used to calculate the needed offset.

offset
Serial number contains vowel -1
Amount of batteries +1 per battery
If serial is even +1
If label CAR is present +1
